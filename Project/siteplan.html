ONE PIECE FAN HUB

Website Planning Document - The Grand Line Awaits!

Site Name

One Piece Fan Hub

This name was chosen to clearly identify the site as a central resource for all things related to the One Piece anime and manga series. The term "Fan Hub" emphasizes the community aspect and the comprehensive nature of the content.

Optional domain availability: onepiecefanhub.com

Site Purpose

The One Piece Fan Hub will serve as the ultimate destination for fans of Eiichiro Oda's epic manga and anime series. The site will provide:

  • Comprehensive character profiles and backstories
  • Detailed information about Devil Fruits and their abilities
  • Interactive maps of the One Piece world (East Blue, Grand Line, New World)
  • Episode and chapter guides with summaries and analysis
  • Latest news about the manga, anime, and related media
  • Community forums for discussion and theory crafting
  • Fan art gallery and cosplay resources

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our target audience that the website will address:

  • What are all the known Devil Fruits and their abilities in the One Piece universe?
  • I'm new to One Piece - where should I start watching and what's the best viewing order?
  • How can I connect with other One Piece fans in my area for viewing parties or events?
  • What are the latest theories about the identity of the final One Piece treasure?
  • Where can I find high-quality images of the Straw Hat crew for wallpapers?
  • What are the differences between the manga and anime adaptations?

Color Schema

The selected color palette is inspired by the One Piece universe, representing the sea, the Straw Hat crew, and treasure:

Primary
#1a237e

Primary Color (#1a237e): Used for headers, navigation, and primary UI elements. This deep blue represents the vast oceans of the One Piece world.

Secondary
#d32f2f

Secondary Color (#d32f2f): Used for buttons, highlights, and important elements. This red represents the Straw Hat Jolly Roger and Luffy's signature color.

Accent
#ffc107

Accent Color (#ffc107): Used for call-to-action elements and decorative accents. This gold represents the treasure sought by pirates throughout the series.

Background
#e3f2fd

Background Color (#e3f2fd): Used for page backgrounds to represent the sky and sea of the One Piece world.

Text
#212121

Text Color (#212121): Used for body text to ensure high contrast and readability.

Typography

The selected fonts capture the adventurous spirit of One Piece while maintaining readability:

Pirata One (Headings)

This decorative serif font with a pirate theme will be used for all headings (H1, H2, H3) to create an immersive One Piece experience.

The King of Pirates Will Be The Freest Person On The Seas!

Roboto (Body Text)

This clean, modern sans-serif font will be used for body text, providing excellent readability across all devices while complementing the heading font.

Sample body text: One Piece follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit.
